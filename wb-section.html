The Opportunity

Bringing South African Products to 100 Million Russian Consumers

Wildberries — rebranded as Wildberries & Russ (RVB) — is Russia's largest e-commerce marketplace with over 100 million active customers and a declared strategy to enter Africa. At SPIEF 2025, CEO Robert Mirzoyan confirmed Africa as the company's next expansion frontier, with South Africa identified as the anchor market for the continent.

Studex Global Markets, through its CEO Tumelo Ramaphosa, met directly with Wildberries leadership in Moscow to explore a dedicated SADC product corridor — starting with Studex Meat's premium biltong, Wagyu and Ankole products, and expanding to broader South African agricultural exports.

The critical enabler is RusQuality — the Russian Government's national quality certification system — which provides the import standards gateway any South African product must pass through to be listed on Wildberries and access Russian consumers.
